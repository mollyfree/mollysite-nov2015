The Cheese Puffs

The world's first Richard Cheese-inspired dance troupe*

*(As far as we know)


Inspired by Broadway musicals, Busby Berkeley films, and middle school dance recitals, the Cheese Puffs were founded in San Francisco in 2008 as tongue-in-cheek interpreters of Richard Cheese's hilarious and expertly arranged lounge covers of pop songs. The Puffs performed until 2011 at various locations on the West Coast, including several live performances with Richard Cheese himself.
